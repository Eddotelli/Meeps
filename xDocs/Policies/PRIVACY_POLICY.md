# Meeps Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

**Last Updated:** January 24, 2026

## Overview

Meeps is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Swedish data protection laws. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

---

## 1. Data Controller

**Organization:** Meeps Event Platform
**Type:** University Project
**Location:** Sweden

---

## 2. Personal Data We Collect

### 2.1 Registration Data

- Email address (required)
- Display name (required)
- Birth date (required for age-restricted events)
- Gender (optional)
- Profile biography (optional)
- Profile image (optional)

### 2.2 Location Data

#### Approximate Location (City-Level)

**What we collect:** Your city and approximate coordinates based on your IP address

**How we collect it:** Automatically upon registration using IP geolocation

**Purpose:** To show you relevant events in your area and improve your experience

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate Interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

**Your rights:**

- ✅ You can view your detected location in Settings > Preferences
- ✅ You can change this location at any time
- ✅ You can use the app without providing location
- ✅ This data is NOT precise location (only city-level)
- ✅ We do NOT track your movements or location history

#### Precise Location (GPS)

**What we collect:** Your exact coordinates (if you enable this feature)

**How we collect it:** Only when you explicitly click "Use my location" or "Events near me"

**Purpose:** To show events very close to you (within a few kilometers)

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

**Your rights:**

- ✅ This is completely optional
- ✅ You will see a clear explanation before your browser asks permission
- ✅ You can revoke permission at any time in your browser settings
- ✅ The app works fully without this feature
- ✅ We do NOT store your precise location
- ✅ We only use it temporarily to show nearby events

### 2.3 Authentication & Security Data

- Password (stored encrypted, never in plain text)
- Email verification tokens (temporary, expires in 24 hours)
- Refresh tokens (for secure login sessions)
- Login timestamps
- IP addresses (for security and location detection)

### 2.4 User Preferences

- Event categories of interest
- Search radius for events
- Preferred location (city)
- Language preference
- Theme preference (dark/light mode)

### 2.5 Event & Activity Data

- Events you create
- Events you join
- Messages you send in event chats

- Data shared: Your IP address (temporarily)

## 6. Event Participation & Messages

### 6.1 When You Leave an Event

When you choose to leave an event:

- ✅ Your event participation is removed
- ✅ Your messages remain visible to other participants
- ✅ Your username and profile remain attached to your messages
- ✅ Other participants' replies and conversations remain intact

**Why?** To preserve conversation history for other participants and event organizers. Removing messages would break conversations and context for others.

**Your options:**

- You can rejoin the event at any time
- You can edit or delete individual messages before leaving
- To remove all your data, you must delete your account (see below)

### 6.2 When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion:

- ✅ All your personal data is removed
- ✅ Your username is replaced with "[Deleted User]"
- ✅ Your messages remain visible but anonymized
- ✅ Event conversations remain intact for other users

**Why?** To preserve conversation context for other participants while removing your personal information. This complies with GDPR Article 17.3 (exceptions to right to erasure) for:

- Freedom of expression and information for others
- Maintaining platform functionality
- Preserving archival records

**What is deleted:**

- Email address
- Password
- Birth date
- Profile image
- Biography
- Location preferences
- All personal settings

**What remains anonymized:**

- Event chat messages (as "[Deleted User]")
- Events you created (transferred to "[Deleted User]")

## 7. Your GDPR Rights

### 7.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)

You can view all your personal data in Settings > Account

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)

You can update your profile information at any time

### 7.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17)

You can request account deletion by contacting us. See Section 6.2 for details on what is deleted vs. anonymized.

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

You can export your data in machine-readable format (feature coming soon)

### 7.5 Right to Object (Art. 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interest (e.g., location detection)

### 7.6 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))

You can change location permissions at any time

### 7.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You can file a complaint with the Swedish Data Protection Authority (Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten - IMY)

**IMY Contact:**

- Website: https://www.imy.se
- Email: imy@imy.se
- Phone: +46 8 657 61 00

## 9. Data Security

### 8.1 Security Measures

- ✅ All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted)
- ✅ Passwords hashed using industry-standard algorithms (ASP.NET Identity)
- ✅ JWT tokens for authentication
- ✅ Secure HTTP-only cookies
- ✅ Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks
- ✅ SQL Server with parameterized queries (prevents SQL injection)
- ✅ Regular security updates

### 8.2 Browser Geolocation

When you use the "Use my location" feature:

- Your browser will ask for permission (required by browser security)
- We only receive coordinates temporarily to show nearby events
- We do NOT store your precise location in our database
- Permission can be revoked in your browser settings

## 14. Technical Details for Transparency

### Location Detection Flow

```
1. User completes registration
2. API detects city from IP address using IP-API.com
3. Stores city name and coordinates in user preferences
4. User can see and change this in Settings
5. No precise location unless user explicitly enables it
```

### What We DON'T Do

- ❌ We don't track your location over time
- ❌ We don't share location with other users without consent
- ❌ We don't use location for advertising
- ❌ We don't access location in the background
- ❌ We don't require precise location to use the app

---

**Summary:** Meeps uses your approximate location (city-level) to show relevant events. This is done automatically using your IP address and is allowed under GDPR as "Legitimate Interest." You can always change this location, and you don't need to provide precise location unless you want to use the "Events near me" feature.
